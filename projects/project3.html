Interactive Sales Dashboard in Tableau

Project Overview

This project analyzes comprehensive sales data from 2011 to 2016, visualizing key performance indicators, trends, and revenue segmentation. The objective was to identify primary revenue drivers, evaluate performance trends, and understand the geographic distribution of sales, and presenting these insights through an interactive dashboard in Tableau.

Performance

    Over the observed period, the business achieved substantial results, generating approximately 85.3 million in total revenue and 32.2 million in profit, derived from 1.35 million orders. This translates to a strong indicative overall profit margin of approximately 37.8%.

Trend Analysis

  • Revenue: Annual revenue exhibited a significant upward trajectory, increasing from approximately $9 million in the initial years to a peak exceeding $20 million in 2015. A subsequent moderate decline was observed in 2016. Notably, a temporary dip occurred between 2013 and 2014 before resuming strong growth.
  • Profit: Annual profit demonstrated consistent year-over-year growth between 2011 and 2015, culminating in a peak exceeding $8 million. Congruent with revenue trends, profit experienced a slight decrease in 2016.

Revenue Composition

    By Product Category: The "Bikes" category stands as the dominant revenue contributor, significantly outpacing other segments and driving the overall revenue peak observed in 2015 (exceeding $15 million for the category). "Accessories" represent the second-largest category, displaying moderate growth, while "Clothing" consistently contributed the smallest revenue share.

    By Geography: Revenue generation is heavily concentrated in North America, identified as the primary market region. Australia constitute significant secondary markets while key European markets (UK, France, and Germany) are at the bottom part.

Concluding Insights

    The analysis highlights a period of strong growth culminating in 2015, followed by a slight contraction in 2016 that warrants further investigation. The business demonstrates a high degree of reliance on the "Bikes" category for revenue generation. Furthermore, sales success is geographically concentrated in North America and select European/Australian markets, indicating potential opportunities for focused growth or strategic market development initiatives.
